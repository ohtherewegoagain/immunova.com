Welcome to Immunova AI

Immunova AI is an open-source initiative advancing immunotherapy through artificial intelligence. We're currently developing Immunova 2.0, a modular multi-modal deep learning framework focused on predicting patient response to immunotherapy and enabling more precise cancer care.

About Immunova 2.0

Immunova 2.0 integrates histopathological images (WSIs), clinical metadata, and genomic information using advanced deep learning models including CNNs, TabNet, and transformers. Our key goals are to:

  • Predict immunotherapy response with multi-modal fusion
  • Analyze survival outcomes using transformer-based survival models
  • Enable interpretability through visual and statistical tools
  • Build an open, clinically relevant research tool accessible to all
